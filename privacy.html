✦ Privacy Policy ✦

Last updated: April 2026

Serenity Quest ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

  • Account information — your email address and password when you create an account.
  • Profile information — your name, birthday, location (optional), and personal "why" statement that you provide during onboarding.
  • Habit & wellness data — the habits you select, your daily check-ins, mood entries, streak counts, and energy levels.
  • Community posts — gratitude posts you share publicly on the Gratitude Wall.
  • Email opt-in preference — whether you have consented to receive educational and wellness communications from us.
  • Technical data — basic usage data such as your zodiac sign (calculated from birthday) and cursor preference.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalise the Serenity Quest experience.
  • To calculate and display your streak, energy, and progress.
  • To show your gratitude posts on the community board.
  • To send wellness and educational emails only if you opted in at signup.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Community Posts

Gratitude posts you share on the Gratitude Wall are visible to all users of the app. Only your first name and general location (if provided) are shown — never your email or full profile. You can delete your own posts at any time.

Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us.
  • You can opt out of marketing emails at any time by contacting us.
  • You can delete your gratitude posts from within the app.

Children

Serenity Quest is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Contact

For any privacy questions or data requests, email us at serenityartnhome@gmail.com.
